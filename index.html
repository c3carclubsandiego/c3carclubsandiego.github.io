About Us.

Welcome to the San Diego Chapter of C3 Car Club. Our club is an international muscle car club for Dodge Challengers, Chevrolet Camaros, and Dodge Chargers, with the purpose of bringing owners together to fellowship in a spirit of camaraderie while providing for and/or enhancing our community.

C3 and the Community.

C3 and the Community.

Here at C3 Car Club we take pride helping our community. We host several community events each year around San Diego County. With a dedicated spirit of unity and cooperation, we are unequivocally committed to become the standard of excellence for community service organizations.

Join Us.

Cars are in our blood; it is a passion that brings us together as a family. Here at C3 we love our cars and our community. If you share our passion and don't have a car club family, you can apply here.

New prospects that wish to join are required to attend at least 3 official events (A meet where the majority of board members are present). Once approved by the board, new prospects will gain admittance into the club and will receive a "C3 Car Club" decal for their car.
